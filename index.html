Exit Between the Days

Developed as part of two artist residencies during 2019 and 2020.

'Exit Between the Days' is a short duration Mask theatre piece using a minimalistic soundtrack, movement cycles.

This piece deals with the sensations of wanting connection and the rituals of the quotidian.  Touching on the themes of aloneness, migration and elderly in society.
